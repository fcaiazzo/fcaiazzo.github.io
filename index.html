I am a proud student living in Somerville, Massachussetts and studying at Tufts University. I'm pursuing a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and expect to graduate in May of 2017. I hope to join the work force as a software engineer working towards more personalized solutions for my clients' problems. I am currently working and training in Watertown, Massachussetts at Central Rock Gym. I pursue my own career as a rock climber every day, but my true passion lies in passing my experience and knowledge down to the next generation. I am the assistant head coach for one of the most talented competitive climbing teams in the greater Boston area and the nation. The athletes that I coach fuel my enthusiasm for everything that I do. From them, I learn to be a better coach, a better climber, and a more influential member of my community.
